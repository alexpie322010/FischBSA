What is Fisch?

Fisch is a Roblox fishing and adventure game where players set sail, catch unique fish, and explore a vast world full of secrets. You can upgrade rods, unlock boats, trade with others, and participate in seasonal events. Legendary bosses lurk beneath the waves, waiting to be discovered by teams of fishers working together. With a thriving economy and endless fish to catch, Fisch delivers an engaging mix of simulator, adventure, and RPG progression.
