What is Fisch?

A strategic fishing RPG experience that transforms classic fishing gameplay with unique bosses, relics, and chaotic admin events.

Fisch is a Roblox adventure game where players explore oceans, lakes, and secret locations to catch powerful creatures, earn rare relics, and upgrade rods. Unlike simple simulators, Fisch combines strategic fishing mechanics, global boss events, and chaotic admin abuse times that bring the whole server together.
